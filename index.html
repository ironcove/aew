PayCore HRIS - Okta Integration

Identity Management System Modernization
Client: All Elite Wrestling (AEW)
Vendor: Iron Cove

Executive Summary

All Elite Wrestling (AEW) is undertaking a strategic identity management modernization initiative focused on integrating Paycor HRIS with Okta to streamline user management and access processes. The group explored automation opportunities through API integration and Okta Workflows, with the goal of simplifying current manual processes and improving identity lifecycle management. The session concluded with a review of Iron Cove's proposed role in providing integration support, a health check of the existing Okta environment, and project planning under a retainer model. This comprehensive engagement will eliminate manual user provisioning processes, establish automated lifecycle management, and create a scalable foundation for future growth while delivering 95% reduction in manual tasks, zero data synchronization errors, and enhanced security posture through role-based access controls.
